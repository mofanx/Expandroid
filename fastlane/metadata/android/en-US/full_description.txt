<p><i>Expandroid</i> lets you send custom messages with a trigger. Want to quickly type out the current date in a specific format? Or do you want your emojis to replace your triggers? You can do it all cross platform with espanso but now on android too with this app!</p><p><br><b>Features supported:</b></p><ul><li>No internet used, maximum privacy.</li><li>Text expander feature with configuration support for the cross platform text expander, "Espanso" on PC.</li><li>Forms support for list, choices and multi-line forms from Espanso.</li><li>Check if your text contains a certain value.</li><li>Convert to upper case or lower case.</li></ul><p><br><b>This app uses the Accessibility API!</b></p><p>This app uses Accessibility API to detect whether a saved shortcut is typed and replace it with a corresponding phrase.</p>
